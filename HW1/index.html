What is a Death Note?

Death Note
A black leather notebook from another dimension, it is used by a creature known as a Shimigami to kill humans.
If fallen into human possession, a human can use the Death Note by writing the name of another human as well as visualizing their face.
After 40 seconds the human whose name has been written shall die of a heart attack, unless within those 40 seconds a specific cause of death is written in afterwards.
